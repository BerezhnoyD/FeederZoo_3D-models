This folder contains the Whole zoo of animal feeders used
in my various behavioral projects over the years. Feel free to explore 
and modify to your needs.
- FED feeder (slightly modified pellet hopper from Markowitz lab/ eventually turned into a disc hopper for ReachOut)
- Homecage_Feeder (copied from another repository, used as an inspiration for own systems)
- ReachOut disc feeder (disc feeder used for out project with motor kinematics, see ReachOut repo or article)
- Ohanes_DIY_Linear_Servo (linear actuator turned precision pellet delivery system by my friend at VAI)
- Operant_Camera MIPT (operant camera and a feeder developed by my friends at MIPT)
- Water_delivery (syringe pump models used for my head-fixed reaching setup, see ReachOut repo)
